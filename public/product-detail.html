NIZZA SHOES
THE CANVAS CLASSIC REISSUED.
Originals brings back a classic with these Nizza Low shoes. Originally conceived as basketball trainers, their clean looks and casual design made them a style hit on the streets.
Like the original, these shoes are made of lightweight, breathable canvas and have a rubber toe bumper and vulcanised outsole. Off-white sidewalls detailed with contrast foxing tape put the finishing touches on this vintage style.
  • Naturally breathable canvas upper
  • Rubber toe bumper
  • Canvas lining
  • Heel pull loop
  • Enjoy the comfort and performance of OrthoLite® sockliner
  • Vulcanised rubber outsole
  • Main materials: Textile and synthetic upper / Textile lining / Rubber outsole
  • Product colour: Core Black / Cloud White / Cloud White
  • Product code: CQ2332
